Our Pillars

Professionalism

Ascend UC Davis holds workshops to help students succeed in the professional world. We have resume workshops to ensure that our members' resumes are ready to send off to potential employers, and excel workshops to teach fundamental skills that are expected at any workplace.


Furthermore, Ascend UC Davis connects students to our national chapter, providing a competitive edge in developing and expanding their network. Ascend's annual National Convention includes a career fair where students can talk to top companies and explore career opportunities in their fields of interest.

Leadership

Ascend UC Davis consists of five committees that members can join - Career Development, Marketing, IT, Professional Development and Student Affairs. In each of these committees, members will have different tasks and projects that they can undertake, providing them the opportunity to develop their leadership abilities through hosting and planning events.
