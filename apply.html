Steps To Apply

  1. If applying as a CM then go to this form and Apply, otherwise skip the next step.
  2. Go to this link and click Join in the navigation bar
  3. Select “Full Time Undergraduate Student” as the type of membership and click Continue
  4. Register with a Username, First Name, Last Name, and select “Students: Univ. of California, Davis”
  5. Accept the Membership and Fill out all accompanied information.
  6. Complete the Billing/Payment Section and send you receipt to ascenducd@gmail.com

Committee Responsibilities

Career Development

  • Keep members up to date on new developments in different career industries
  • Provide career guidance and development to members
  • Encourage members to connect with professionals in different industries during networking events

Professional Development

  • Develop communication skills through talking and coordinating with guest speakers
  • Increase networking awareness through attending and holding various networking events
  • Create a unique connection network for Ascend UC Davis by reaching out to professionals across various industries

Marketing

  • Promote Ascend UCD as a premier leadership organization within the Davis community and in the business field in order to recruit more members and increase member participation
  • Utilize social media, such as Facebook and Instagram, to reach a wide range of audience

Student Affairs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ascend Leadership?

Ascend Leadership is the largest, non-profit Pan-Asian organization for business professionals in North America. It offer robust professional development and career enhancement programs, enabling emerging leaders to pursue their dreams and achieve professional heights.

What are some benefits?

Members of Ascend UC Davis can attend business networking events, meet students from different chapters of Ascend, improve their professional side, and much more. It is also a community of like-minded people who are supportive of one another.

Do I need to be Pan-Asian to join?

No, all races and ethnicities are welcome!

What is the time commitment?

There is no time commitment for general members, but most general members attend meetings once a week.

Do I need to be a Managerial Economics/Econmics major to join?

No, we welcome all majors. We have majors in Communications, Statistics, Computer Science, Film, and many others.

Is attending all meetings and events required?

If you are a general member, there are no requirements! You can come network and attend any open events whenever you have time.
